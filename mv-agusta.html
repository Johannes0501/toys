The Naked Icon

The MV Agusta Brutale 800 is more than just a motorcycle; it's a statement of design and performance. With its signature trellis frame in red and aggressive stance, it redefines the naked bike segment.

Powered by a counter-rotating three-cylinder engine, it delivers a unique sound and handling character that is unmistakably Italian. This 2017 model features the updated design language with the hollow tail section and full LED lighting.
